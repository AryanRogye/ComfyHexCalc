# Privacy Policy for Hex Calculator

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Hex Calculator ("the app") is built to run locally on your Mac. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data.

## Data Collection

Hex Calculator does **not** collect personal information.

Hex Calculator does **not** use:
- user accounts
- analytics SDKs
- advertising SDKs
- tracking technologies
- third-party data brokers

## Data Stored on Your Device

To provide core functionality, Hex Calculator stores a small amount of data locally on your Mac using Apple system storage (`UserDefaults`):
- calculation history (recent entries)
- app preference settings (for example, float-on-top and dock visibility preferences)

This data remains on your device and is not sent to us.

## Clipboard Access

Hex Calculator can read clipboard text only when you explicitly paste into the app (for example, using Command+V), and can write values to clipboard only when you explicitly copy a result.

## Data Sharing

We do not share data with third parties.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Because app data is stored locally, you control retention:
- You can clear history from inside the app.
- You can remove all app data by deleting the app and its local preferences from your Mac.

## Children's Privacy

Hex Calculator is a general utility app and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date.
